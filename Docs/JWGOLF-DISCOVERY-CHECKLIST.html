JW Golf — Discovery Checklist

Purpose: Understand what's already in place before deciding what to build/change

Current Website

  • Platform: WordPress? Wix? Custom? Other?
  • What's working? Any pages that perform well?
  • What's NOT working? Outdated info? Things you hate?
  • Domain: Where registered? When renews?
  • Hosting: Who hosts? What paying? Any issues?

Booking System

  • What do you use? Calendly? Acuity? Phone/email? Other?
  • How well does it work? Integrates with website? Friction for clients?
  • Payment: Take online payment? Deposits? Cash on day?
  • Lessons/packages: List all with prices, duration, location

Email & Marketing

  • Email list: How many contacts? What platform?
  • Newsletters: How often? What content?
  • Website signup: Is there a form? What's the offer?

Social Media

  • Platforms: Facebook? Instagram? YouTube? TikTok? LinkedIn?
  • Activity: How often do you post? What works? What flops?
  • Followers: Rough numbers per platform
  • Video content: Any YouTube? Reels? TikToks?

Brand Assets

  • Logo: Do you have one? Like it? Have original files?
  • Colours/fonts: Consistent? Like them?
  • Photos: Professional photos? When taken? Can send?
  • Video: Any coaching videos? Testimonials on video?

Testimonials & Social Proof

  • Written testimonials: Where? How many?
  • Video testimonials: Any from happy clients?
  • Google Business: Claimed? How many reviews?
  • Happy clients: Any who'd be great case studies?

Clients & Positioning

  • Current clients: Beginners? Mid-handicappers? Low? Age range?
  • How they find you: Word of mouth? Google? Social?
  • Ideal client: Who do you enjoy most? Who pays best?
  • Current pricing: Per lesson? Packages? What's included?
  • Ideal pricing: What would you LIKE to charge?

The Story (Critical)

  • Epilepsy story comfort (1-10): _____
  • How to use it: Full website feature / Brief mention / Not at all
  • Talked about it publicly before? Social media? Articles?
  • Epilepsy charity connections? Would you be open to that?

Technical Comfort

  • Tech comfort (1-10): _____
  • Manage own website? Or pay someone?
  • Preference: We manage everything / You're involved day-to-day / Learn yourself
  • Communication: Email? WhatsApp? Phone? How often?
