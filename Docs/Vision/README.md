# JW Golf Vision Documents

Vision statements, goals, and requirements for the JW Golf website project.

## Core Vision

**Position James Whittaker as "The Resilient Coach"** — a brand built on authentic struggle, clarity of purpose, and proven results.

## Key Differentiators

1. **The Resilience Story** — 5 brain surgeries for epilepsy
2. **The Philosophy** — "One Clear Fix" methodology
3. **The Credibility** — 50+ European Tour pros fitted, Titleist Ambassador

## Business Goals

- Move from £40-60/hour to £100+/hour premium positioning
- Build recurring revenue through online coaching subscriptions
- Establish passive income through digital products

## Target Revenue

- **Year 1:** £40k → £156k (290% increase)
- **Year 2:** Scale online coaching to 500+ subscribers
- **Year 3:** Passive income exceeds active coaching income
