1. Brand Positioning

The Core Story: "Resilience Through Clarity"

The Narrative Arc James faced life-threatening epilepsy requiring 5 brain surgeries. He learned that recovery happens one step at a time — one day, one exercise, one victory. He applies this to golf: you don't need 20 swing thoughts, you need ONE clear fix. His coaching is trauma-informed, patient, and focused on progress over perfection.

Why This Works Authentically

  • The epilepsy story demonstrates mental resilience — the #1 skill in golf
  • It shows he's overcome adversity most people can't imagine
  • It positions him as someone who understands struggle and incremental progress
  • It's inspiring without being self-pitying
  • It differentiates him from every other "PGA Professional" in Yorkshire

8. Competitor Analysis

What Top UK Golf Coaches Are Doing

Mark Crossfield

  • Model: Freemium → Paid courses → Membership
  • Differentiation: "Real instruction, no gimmicks"
  • Revenue: Courses, membership, affiliate commissions

Me and My Golf

  • Model: Subscription tiers (Core £10.75/mo, VIP £54/mo)
  • Differentiation: Structured coaching plans, community
  • Revenue: Subscriptions, partnerships, merchandise

Rick Shiels

  • Model: YouTube ad revenue + sponsorships
  • Differentiation: Personality-driven, equipment reviews
  • Revenue: YouTube, brand deals, golf stores
Key Insight: None of the major UK coaches are telling a personal resilience story. James's epilepsy journey is a completely unique differentiator in a crowded market.
