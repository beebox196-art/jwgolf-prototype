# JW Golf — Discovery Questions
## What We Need to Know Before Planning

**Purpose:** Understand existing assets before deciding what to build/change

---

## Current Website

### What We Need to Ask:
1. **What platform is it on?**
   - WordPress? Wix? Custom? Other?
   - Do you have login access?

2. **What's working well?**
   - Are people finding it through search?
   - Do you get inquiries from it?
   - Any pages that perform well?

3. **What's NOT working?**
   - Pages that never get visited?
   - Outdated information?
   - Things you hate about it?

4. **Do you own the domain?**
   - Where is it registered?
   - When does it renew?

5. **Hosting situation?**
   - Who hosts it?
   - What are you paying?
   - Any issues with it?

---

## Booking System

### What We Need to Ask:
1. **What do you use now?**
   - Calendly? Acuity? Phone/email? Something else?

2. **How well does it work?**
   - Does it integrate with your website?
   - Do clients book without friction?
   - Are you happy with it?

3. **Payment processing?**
   - Do you take payment online?
   - Cash/transfer on the day?
   - Do you want to take deposits?

4. **What lessons/packages do you offer?**
   - List them all with prices
   - Duration, location, what's included

---

## Email & Marketing

### What We Need to Ask:
1. **Do you have an email list?**
   - How many contacts?
   - What platform? (Mailchimp, ConvertKit, nothing yet?)

2. **Do you send newsletters?**
   - How often?
   - What content?
   - Open rates/click rates?

3. **Do you collect emails on your website?**
   - Is there a signup form?
   - What's the offer for signing up?

---

## Social Media

### What We Need to Ask:
1. **What platforms are you on?**
   - Facebook? Instagram? Twitter/X? LinkedIn? YouTube? TikTok?

2. **How active are you?**
   - How often do you post?
   - What content works well?
   - What flops?

3. **Do you have followers?**
   - Rough numbers per platform
   - Are they local golfers? Pros? General public?

4. **Do you have video content?**
   - YouTube channel?
   - Instagram Reels/TikToks?
   - Any content you're proud of?

---

## Brand Assets

### What We Need to Ask:
1. **Do you have a logo?**
   - Do you like it?
   - Do you have the original files?

2. **Brand colours/fonts?**
   - Are they consistent?
   - Do you like them?

3. **Professional photos?**
   - Do you have them?
   - When were they taken?
   - Can you send them to me?

4. **Video content?**
   - Any coaching videos?
   - Testimonials on video?
   - What do you have that we can use?

---

## Testimonials & Social Proof

### What We Need to Ask:
1. **Do you have written testimonials?**
   - Where are they? (website, Google, Facebook?)
   - How many?

2. **Any video testimonials?**
   - From happy clients?

3. **Google Business Profile?**
   - Do you have one?
   - How many reviews?
   - Is it claimed and updated?

4. **Can we contact happy clients?**
   - Would you be comfortable asking for testimonials?
   - Any clients who'd be great case studies?

---

## Clients & Positioning

### What We Need to Ask:
1. **Who are your current clients?**
   - Beginners? Mid-handicappers? Low handicappers?
   - Age range? Gender split?
   - Local or travel to you?

2. **How do clients find you?**
   - Word of mouth? Google? Social media? Clubs?

3. **What's your ideal client?**
   - Who do you enjoy coaching most?
   - Who pays the best rates?
   - Who's easiest to work with?

4. **What's your current pricing?**
   - Per lesson?
   - Packages?
   - What's included?

5. **What would you LIKE to charge?**
   - If you could set ideal pricing
   - What's stopping you?

---

## The Story

### What We Need to Ask:
1. **How comfortable are you sharing the epilepsy journey?**
   - On a scale of 1-10?
   - Full story on website? Brief mention? Not at all?

2. **Have you talked about it publicly before?**
   - Social media? Articles? Interviews?

3. **Are you connected to any epilepsy charities?**
   - Would you be open to that?

4. **What's the headline version of your story?**
   - "5 brain surgeries and still coaching"
   - "Resilience through golf"
   - Something else?

---

## Technical Comfort

### What We Need to Ask:
1. **How do you feel about technology?**
   - Comfortable? Overwhelmed? Somewhere in between?

2. **Do you manage your own website?**
   - Or do you pay someone?

3. **Would you prefer to:**
   - Have us manage everything and you just approve?
   - Be involved day-to-day?
   - Learn to manage some things yourself?

4. **How do you prefer to communicate?**
   - Email? WhatsApp? Phone calls?
   - How often?
